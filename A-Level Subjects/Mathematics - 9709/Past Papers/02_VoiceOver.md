# Voice-Over Script: Using Past Papers (Cambridge International AS & A Level Mathematics 9709)

**[Visual: 9709 paper stack — Pure, Mechanics, Statistics labels]**

**Narrator:**  
**Mathematics 9709** rewards **method** as much as **answers**. **Mark schemes** spell out **M** marks for **valid** **steps** and **A** marks for **accuracy**—**show** **working** that **matches** the **expected** **route**.

---

**[Visual: “Hence” in bold red on question stem]**

**Narrator:**  
When a question says **hence**, the **examiner** wants the **previous** result **used**. **Starting again** from **scratch** can **cost** marks even if you reach the **right** **answer**.

---

**[Visual: Calculator MODE button — Deg vs Rad]**

**Narrator:**  
**Trigonometry** in **calculus** is almost always **radians**. **Mechanics** may use **degrees** for **angles** in **triangles**—read the **question** and **reset** **MODE** **deliberately**.

---

**[Visual: Stopwatch — 1 mark per minute rule of thumb]**

**Narrator:**  
**Pace** yourself: if you’re **stuck**, **bank** the **partial** work and **move**. **Return** with **fresh** eyes if **time** allows. **Easy** marks hide at the **end** of **long** papers.

---

**[Visual: Formula booklet tabs]**

**Narrator:**  
Your **formula booklet** is **allowed** in the **exam**—**know** **where** **binomial**, **normal**, and **integration** formulas live so you **don’t** **hunt** under **stress**.

---

**[Visual: Error log — “sign error”, “forgot +c”]**

**Narrator:**  
After each **mock**, **log** **repeat** mistakes. **Integration** **constant**, **domain** of **inverse** **functions**, **direction** in **vectors**—**fix** **patterns**, not **individual** **papers**.

---

**[Visual: Checklist — method marks, hence, time, booklet, review]**

**Narrator:**  
**Past papers** are **training data** for your **brain**. **Mark** **honestly**, **fix** **fast**, **repeat**. Good luck.
