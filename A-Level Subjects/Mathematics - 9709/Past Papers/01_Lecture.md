# Complete Lecture: Using Past Papers (Cambridge International AS & A Level Mathematics 9709)

**Examination technique** for **9709**—how to **translate** skill into **marks**. Official hub: [Cambridge International AS & A Level Mathematics (9709)](https://www.cambridgeinternational.org/programmes-and-qualifications/cambridge-international-as-and-a-level-mathematics-9709/).

---

## 1. Know your route

**9709** offers **Pure only**, **Pure + Mechanics**, or **Pure + Probability & Statistics** combinations. **Confirm** which **Papers 4–6** you sit and their **time** / **mark** allocation from your **current** **syllabus** and **entry**.

---

## 2. Mark scheme habits

- **Method (M)** marks: **correct** **approach** even if **arithmetic** slips.  
- **Accuracy (A)** marks: **dependent** on **earning** **M** where **stated**.  
- **Independent (B)** marks: **statements**, **diagrams**, **conclusions**.  
- **Follow-through (ft):** **error** carried **forward** from **earlier** part — **use** their **wrong** value **consistently** in **later** **stages**.

**Always show sufficient working** for **M** marks — **“answer only”** may score **zero**.

---

## 3. Command words

- **Show that:** **prove** result — **every** **logical** step.  
- **Hence:** **must** use **previous** part — **penalty** if **ignored**.  
- **In exact form:** **surds** / **fractions** / **π** — **no** **rounded** **decimals** unless **allowed**.  
- **Sketch:** **shape**, **key** **points**, **asymptotes**, **intercepts** — **not** **plotting** **every** dot.

---

## 4. Calculator discipline

- **Radians** vs **degrees** — **check** **mode** **every** **trig** question.  
- **Store** **intermediate** values **full precision**; **round** **only** **final** answer to **requested** **s.f.**

---

## 5. Time management

- **Rough** guide: **1 mark ≈ 1–1.2 minutes** on **many** papers — **adjust** after **timed** **practice**.  
- **Stuck** > **3 minutes?** **Flag** and **move** — **easier** marks **later**.  
- **Leave** **5 minutes** to **check** **pure** **algebra** **signs** and **statistics** **tails**.

---

## 6. Structured revision cycle

1. **Timed** **full** paper.  
2. **Mark** with **scheme**; **highlight** **lost** **M** vs **A** marks.  
3. **Redo** **only** **failed** questions **next** day **without** notes.  
4. **Maintain** **error log** (**topics** + **mistake type**: **sign**, **domain**, **calculator**).

---

## 7. Formula booklet

**Tab** pages you use **often** (**trig**, **series**, **normal**, **integration**) — **speed** under **pressure**.

---

## 8. Presentation

- **Equal signs** connect **equal** **quantities** only.  
- **Vector** notation **clear** (underlines or **bold** as **accepted**).  
- **Diagram** for **vectors**, **mechanics**, **geometry** **before** **algebra**.

---

## Checklist before the exam

- [ ] **Two** **full** **papers** this week under **exam** **conditions**.  
- [ ] **Hence** questions **practised** **consciously**.  
- [ ] **Calculator** **reset** / **mode** **checked** **morning** of exam.  
- [ ] **Sleep** — **accuracy** beats **last-minute** **cramming**.

**Now close this file and start a **timed** **Pure** paper.**
