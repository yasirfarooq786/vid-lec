## 1. Basic laws

**P(A)** ∈ **[0, 1]**; **P(not A) = 1 − P(A)**

**Mutually exclusive:** **P(A ∩ B) = 0** → **P(A ∪ B) = P(A) + P(B)**

**General addition:** **P(A ∪ B) = P(A) + P(B) − P(A ∩ B)**

---

## 2. Conditional probability

**P(A | B) = P(A ∩ B) / P(B)** provided **P(B) > 0**

**Multiplication:** **P(A ∩ B) = P(A) × P(B|A) = P(B) × P(A|B)**

---

## 3. Independence

**A**, **B** **independent** ⟺ **P(A ∩ B) = P(A)P(B)** ⟺ **P(A|B) = P(A)**

**Do not** assume independence without **justification** from **question**.

---

## 4. Tree diagrams

**Multiply** along **branches** for **joint** probabilities; **add** **disjoint** **paths** for **OR**.

---

## 5. Counting

**Permutations** (order matters): **n! / (n−r)!** for **r** from **n** distinct; **adjust** for **repetitions**.

**Combinations** (order irrelevant): **ⁿCᵣ = n! / (r!(n−r)!)**

**Arrangements** with **repeated** objects: **n! / (n₁! n₂! …)**

---

## 6. Discrete probability distributions

**Probability function** **p(x)** with **Σ p(x) = 1**

**Expectation** **E(X) = Σ x p(x)**  
**Var(X) = E(X²) − [E(X)]²**

---

## 7. Binomial distribution **B(n, p)**

**n** **independent** trials, **constant** **p** success:

**P(X = r) = ⁿCᵣ pʳ (1−p)ⁿ⁻ʳ**

**E(X) = np**, **Var(X) = np(1−p)**

---

## 8. Geometric (where examined)

**First** success on **k**th trial: **P = (1−p)ᵏ⁻¹p**

---

## Common mistakes

- **Double-counting** in **union** problems.  
- **Confusing** **P(A|B)** with **P(B|A)** (**Bayes** carefully).  
- **With** vs **without** **replacement** — **changes** **conditional** probabilities.
