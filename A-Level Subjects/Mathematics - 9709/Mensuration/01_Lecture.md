# Complete Lecture: Mensuration (Cambridge International AS & A Level Mathematics 9709)

**Mensuration** = **measurement** of **length**, **area**, and **volume**—including **radian** measure (**circular measure**) and **standard solids**. Strongly linked to **integration** for **volumes of revolution** in **Pure**. Programme: [Cambridge 9709](https://www.cambridgeinternational.org/programmes-and-qualifications/cambridge-international-as-and-a-level-mathematics-9709/).

---

## 1. Radians

**π radians = 180°**  
**θ rad = θ × (180/π)°**; **θ° = θ × (π/180) rad**

**Why radians:** **calculus** formulas (**d/dx sin x = cos x**) hold when **x** is in **radians**.

---

## 2. Arc length and sector area

**Radius r**, **angle θ** (radians) at centre:

- **Arc length** **s = rθ**  
- **Sector area** **A = ½ r²θ**

If **θ** given in **degrees**, convert **first** or use **degree** versions: **s = 2πr × (θ/360)**, etc.

---

## 3. Plane shapes (revision)

| Shape | Area |
|-------|------|
| **Triangle** | **½ × base × height** = **½ ab sin C** |
| **Trapezium** | **½(a + b)h** |
| **Parallelogram** | **base × height** |
| **Circle** | **πr²**; **circumference 2πr** |

---

## 4. Common solids

| Solid | Volume | Surface area (where useful) |
|-------|--------|----------------------------|
| **Cuboid** | **lwh** | **2(lw + lh + wh)** |
| **Cylinder** | **πr²h** | **curved 2πrh** + **circles** |
| **Cone** | **⅓πr²h** | |
| **Sphere** | **(4/3)πr³** | **4πr²** |
| **Pyramid** | **⅓ × base area × height** | |

**Frustum** (truncated cone): **difference** of **two** cones or **formula** from **question**.

---

## 5. Solids of revolution (Pure extension)

**y = f(x)** rotated **360°** about **x-axis** from **x = a** to **b:**

**V = π ∫[a,b] y² dx**

About **y-axis** (or **shell** methods in advanced courses) — follow **syllabus** **method**.

---

## 6. Related rates and optimisation

**Chain rule** links **dV/dt** to **dr/dt** for **expanding** **sphere**, etc. (synoptic with **differentiation**).

---

## Common mistakes

- Using **degree** **θ** in **s = rθ** without **conversion**.  
- **Confusing** **diameter** and **radius**.  
- **Volume** of **cone** missing **factor one third**.
