# Voice-Over Script: Geometry (Cambridge International AS & A Level Mathematics 9709)

**[Visual: Parallel lines Z-angle alternate]**

**Narrator:**  
**Geometry** starts with **angle facts**. **Parallel** lines cut by a **transversal** create **matching** **corresponding** angles and **equal** **alternate** angles—perfect for **angle chasing** without **algebra** yet.

---

**[Visual: Similar triangles — scale factor k]**

**Narrator:**  
**Similar** shapes have **equal** **angles** and **proportional** **sides**. If **lengths** scale by **k**, **areas** scale by **k squared** and **volumes** by **k cubed**. That rule rescues **many** **ratio** problems.

---

**[Visual: Circle theorems — angle at centre double angle at circumference]**

**Narrator:**  
**Circle theorems** turn **symmetry** into **equalities**. The **angle at the centre** is **twice** the **angle at the circumference** standing on the **same** **arc**. A **tangent** meets a **radius** at **ninety degrees**.

---

**[Visual: Right triangle — Pythagoras a²+b²=c²]**

**Narrator:**  
**Pythagoras** is **only** for **right** triangles. If you need **area** with **two** sides and **included** angle, switch to **half a b sine C** from **trigonometry**.

---

**[Visual: Checklist — angles, similarity, circles, area links]**

**Narrator:**  
When a diagram looks **messy**, **label** **everything** you **know** and work **one** **triangle** at a time. Next: **mensuration** with **radians** and **volumes**.
