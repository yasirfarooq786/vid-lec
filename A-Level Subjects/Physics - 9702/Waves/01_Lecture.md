## 1. Progressive waves

**Displacement** of **medium** or **field** varies **periodically** with **time** and **position**.

- **Amplitude A:** **maximum** **displacement** from **equilibrium**.  
- **Period T:** **time** for **one** **cycle**.  
- **Frequency f:** **cycles per second** (**Hz**); **f = 1/T**.  
- **Wavelength λ:** **distance** between **successive** **in-phase** points on **same** wave.

**Wave speed:** **v = fλ** (for **all** progressive waves in **steady** medium).

**Phase difference:** **one** **wavelength** = **360°** or **2π rad**; **path difference** links to **interference**.

**Longitudinal** vs **transverse:** **particle** **motion** **parallel** vs **perpendicular** to **energy** **transfer**.

---

## 2. Electromagnetic spectrum

**Transverse** **travelling** waves; **speed c** in **vacuum** **≈ 3.00 × 10⁸ m s⁻¹** (data sheet).

**Order** (increasing **f** / decreasing **λ**): radio → microwave → infrared → visible → ultraviolet → X-ray → gamma.

**Production** and **detection** **vary** by **region** (syllabus **details**).

---

## 3. Superposition and interference

**Principle of superposition:** **resultant** **displacement** = **sum** of **individual** **displacements**.

**Coherent** sources: **constant** **phase** **relationship** (usually **same f**).

**Constructive:** path difference **= nλ** (for **two** **in-phase** sources, **central** **maximum** at **equal** path).  
**Destructive:** path difference **= (n + ½)λ** (for **equal** **amplitude** **cancellation**).

**Young double-slit** (qualitative + quantitative): **λ = ax / D** (**a** slit **separation**, **D** screen **distance**, **x** fringe **spacing**) — **check** **notation** on **your** **formula** **sheet**.

---

## 4. Diffraction

**Spreading** when **wave** meets **gap** or **obstacle**. **Significant** diffraction when **gap** **width** **comparable** to **λ**.

---

## 5. Stationary (standing) waves

**Superposition** of **two** **progressive** waves **same** **v**, **f**, **λ**, **equal** **A**, **opposite** **direction**.

**Nodes:** **zero** **amplitude**; **antinodes:** **maximum** **amplitude**.

**String** **fixed** **ends:** **length** **L = n(λ/2)** for **nth** **harmonic**; **fundamental** **n = 1**.

**Open** **pipe** **both** **ends:** **similar** **harmonic** **series**; **closed** **one** **end** differs (**syllabus** **patterns**).

---

## 6. Measuring waves

**Oscilloscope:** **timebase** → **period**; **Y-gain** → **voltage** **amplitude** (if **applied** to **wave**).

---

## Common misconceptions

- **Particles** do **not** **travel** **one** **wavelength** **per** **period** in **transverse** **mechanical** waves—**energy** **propagates**, **medium** **oscillates**.  
- **Path difference** **alone** does **not** give **constructive** **unless** **sources** **coherent** **and** **phase** **accounted** **for**.
