# Voice-Over Script: Thermal Physics (Cambridge International AS & A Level Physics 9702)

**[Visual: Two blocks in contact — arrows until same T]**

**Narrator:**  
**Temperature** **drives** **net** **energy** **flow**. At **thermal** **equilibrium**, **that** **flow** **stops**—**same** **temperature**, **not** **necessarily** **same** **internal** **energy**.

---

**[Visual: Beaker on heater — Q, m, ΔT on labels]**

**Narrator:**  
**Specific** **heat** **capacity** **links** **temperature** **rise** to **energy** **added** **per** **kilogram**. **Watch** **units**: **joules**, **kilograms**, **kelvin** **or** **celsius** **intervals**—**intervals** **match**.

---

**[Visual: Ice melting — flat T vs time plateau]**

**Narrator:**  
During **melting** or **boiling**, **energy** **still** **enters** but **temperature** **can** **stay** **flat**. That’s **latent** **heat**—**breaking** **or** **overcoming** **intermolecular** **arrangements**, **not** **speeding** **up** **molecules** **on** **average** **yet**.

---

**[Visual: pV diagram for gas — piston animation]**

**Narrator:**  
The **ideal** **gas** **equation** **p V equals n R T** **packages** **macroscopic** **variables**. **Ideal** **means** **simple** **collisions** and **negligible** **molecular** **volume** **compared** **to** **container**—a **model**, **not** **all** **real** **fluids**.

---

**[Visual: Distribution of molecular speeds — T low vs high]**

**Narrator:**  
**Higher** **temperature** **means** **higher** **typical** **random** **kinetic** **energy**. **Root-mean-square** **speed** **summarises** that **spread** for **calculations** **linking** **pressure** **and** **density**.

---

**[Visual: Checklist — heat vs T, c and L, pV=nRT, kinetic theory]**

**Narrator:**  
Always **state** **what** **is** **held** **constant** in a **process**—**constant** **volume** **heating** **behaves** **differently** from **constant** **pressure** **expansion**. Next: **nuclear physics**.
