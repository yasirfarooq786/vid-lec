# Complete Lecture: Electricity and Magnetism (Cambridge International AS & A Level Physics 9702)

## 1. Current, potential difference, and resistance

**Current I:** **rate** of **flow** of **charge**, **I = Q/t** (**A** = **C s⁻¹**).

**PD V** across **conductor:** **energy** **per** **unit** **charge** transferred; **V = W/Q**.

**Ohm’s law** (ohmic **materials**): **V = IR**.

**Resistivity:** **R = ρL/A** (**geometry** + **material**).

**Power:** **P = VI = I²R = V²/R**.

---

## 2. Kirchhoff’s laws

**Junction rule:** **ΣI in = ΣI out** (conservation of **charge**).

**Loop rule:** **Σ EMFs = Σ IR** around **closed** **loop** (**signs** from **direction** **chosen**).

**Series:** **R total = R₁ + R₂ + …**; **same I**.  
**Parallel:** **1/R total = 1/R₁ + 1/R₂ + …**; **same V** across **each** **branch**.

---

## 3. EMF and internal resistance

**Terminal PD:** **V = E − Ir** (**E** EMF, **r** **internal** **resistance**, **I** **drawn**).

**Power** **dissipated** in **r** reduces **useful** **output** **unless** **matched** **load** (applications).

---

## 4. Capacitors

**C = Q/V** (**farad**). **Energy** stored: **W = ½ QV = ½ CV² = Q²/(2C)**.

**Series / parallel** **rules** (inverse **pattern** to **resistors**).

**RC** **charging** / **discharging** — **exponential** **graphs**; **time constant τ = RC**.

---

## 5. Electric fields

**Coulomb** **force** between **point** **charges** (inverse **square**); **field** **E = F/Q** (**N C⁻¹** or **V m⁻¹**).

**Uniform** **field** between **parallel** **plates:** **E = V/d** (magnitude).

**Motion** of **charged** **particle** in **fields** — **projectile** **or** **circular** **components** depending on **geometry**.

---

## 6. Magnetic fields and forces

**Flux density B** (**tesla**); **Fleming’s left-hand rule** for **motor** **effect**.

**Straight** **conductor:** **F = BIL sin θ** (**I** ⟂ **B** gives **maximum**).

**Moving** **charge:** **F = Bqv sin θ**; **circular** **path** when **v** ⟂ **uniform** **B**.

---

## 7. Electromagnetic induction

**Magnetic flux Φ = BA cos θ**; **flux linkage NΦ** for **N** **turns**.

**Faraday’s law:** **induced EMF** **magnitude** **∝ rate of change** of **(NΦ)**.

**Lenz’s law:** **direction** **opposes** **change** causing it — **conservation** **of** **energy**.

**Transformer** (ideal): **Vₚ/Vₛ = Nₚ/Nₛ**; **power** **considerations**.

---

## Common misconceptions

- **PD** **across** **open** **circuit** **terminals** **≠** **EMF** when **I > 0** and **r > 0**.  
- **Capacitor** **current** **with** **d.c.** **steady** state → **0** (fully **charged**).  
- **Motor** vs **dynamo** rules — **different** **energy** **conversion** **direction**.
