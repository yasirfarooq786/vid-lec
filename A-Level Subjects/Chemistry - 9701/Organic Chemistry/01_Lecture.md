## Homologous series and nomenclature (IUPAC)

- **Root** = longest **carbon** **chain**; **suffix** = **functional group**; **prefix** = **substituents** (**locants** = **position** numbers, **lowest** set rule).  
- **Alkane** **-ane**, **alkene** **-ene**, **alcohol** **-ol**, **aldehyde** **-al**, **ketone** **-one**, **carboxylic acid** **-oic acid**, **halogenoalkane** **halo-** prefix.

---

## Structural isomerism

**Chain**, **positional**, **functional group** isomers (e.g. **propanal** vs **propanone**).

## Stereoisomerism

- **Cis–trans** (restricted **C=C** rotation; **two different** groups on **each** **sp²** carbon).  
- **Optical isomerism** (**chiral** **centre** with **four different** groups; **non-superimposable mirror images**).

---

## Alkanes

**Free-radical substitution** with **halogens** (**UV**); **chain** **propagation** / **initiation** / **termination** steps.

---

## Alkenes

**Electrophilic addition** of **HBr**, **Br₂** (**mechanism**: **π** **electron** **attack** on **electrophile**, **carbocation** **intermediate** in **asymmetric** cases—**Markovnikov** rule for **HX**).

**Test:** **bromine water** **decolourises**.

**Oxidative cleavage** / **diol** formation with **cold dilute MnO₄⁻** (conditions determine **product**).

---

## Halogenoalkanes

**Nucleophilic substitution:** **OH⁻** → **alcohol**; **CN⁻** → **nitrile** (+**C chain**); **NH₃** → **amine** (stepwise).

**Mechanisms:** **SN1** (carbocation, **tertiary** favours) vs **SN2** (**backside attack**, **primary** favours)—**rate** **laws** and **steric** effects.

**Elimination** with **ethanolic** **OH⁻** → **alkene** (**Zaitsev** preference **awareness**).

---

## Alcohols

**Oxidation:** **primary** → **aldehyde** → **carboxylic acid** (distillation vs **reflux**); **secondary** → **ketone**; **tertiary** **resistant** to **acidified** **dichromate**.

**Dehydration** (**conc H₂SO₄**) → **alkene**.

**Esterification** with **carboxylic acids** (**conc H₂SO₄** **catalyst**).

---

## Carbonyl compounds

**Aldehydes** **oxidised** by **Tollens’** / **Fehling’s**; **ketones** **not** (except **α-hydroxy** **exceptions** at **A2** depth if required).

**Nucleophilic addition** of **HCN** (**cyanohydrin**), **2,4-DNPH** **identification** (**orange** **precipitate**).

---

## Carboxylic acids and derivatives

**Acid** + **base** → **salt** + **water**; **with alcohol** → **ester** + **water**.

**Acyl chlorides** / **anhydrides**—**faster** **acylation** than **acid** (mechanism **awareness**).

**Hydrolysis** of **esters** (**acid** or **alkali** **catalysed** routes).

---

## Benzene (aromatic) chemistry

**Delocalised** **π** **system**; **electrophilic substitution** (**nitration**, **halogenation** with **halogen carrier**, **Friedel–Crafts** **awareness**). **Resistant** to **addition** that would **destroy** **aromaticity**.

**Phenol:** **ring** **activated**; **bromine water** → **tribromophenol** **precipitate**.

---

## Nitrogen-containing organics

**Amines** **basicity** (**alkyl** > **NH₃** > **aryl** **qualitative**); **amides** from **acyl** + **ammonia**/**amine**.

**Amino acids** **zwitterions**; **peptide** **link** (**amide** bond).

---

## Polymers

**Addition** (**polyalkene** from **alkene**); **condensation** (**polyester**, **polyamide**—**repeat unit** drawing).

**Biodegradable** / **recycling** issues **synoptic** with **environment**.

---

## Analytical links

**Infrared:** **characteristic** **bond** **stretches** (e.g. **C=O**, **O–H**).  
**Mass spectrometry:** **molecular ion**, **fragmentation**.  
**NMR** (**13C** / **¹H** at **A Level** depth): **chemical shift**, **splitting** (**n+1** rule for **adjacent** **non-equivalent** **H**).

## Common misconceptions

- **Curly arrows** start from **bonds** or **lone pairs**, **not** atoms **without** **electrons**.  
- **Optical activity** requires **chiral** **molecule**; **meso** compounds have **internal** **plane** **symmetry**.
