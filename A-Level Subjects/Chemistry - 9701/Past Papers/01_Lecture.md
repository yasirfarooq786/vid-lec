## Command words

- **Define / state:** **short**, **precise**.  
- **Describe:** **what** you **see** or **what** **happens** (**observations**, **trends**).  
- **Explain:** **why**—**cause** and **effect** with **chemistry** (**particles**, **bonds**, **energy**, **equilibrium**).  
- **Suggest:** **reasonable** **hypothesis** from **data** given.  
- **Calculate:** **show working**; **units** and **significant figures** as **stated**.

---

## Calculations

- **Moles** **bridge** for **all** **stoichiometry**.  
- **Rearrange** **pV = nRT** **carefully**; **watch** **dm³** vs **m³**.  
- **ΔH** cycles: **label** **arrows** in **Hess** diagrams.  
- **Equilibrium** **Kc** / **Kp**: **homogeneous** **units**; **ICE** tables.

---

## Organic answers

- **Displayed** formulae: **every** **heteroatom** and **H** on **functional** **carbon** **shown** when asked.  
- **Mechanisms:** **curly arrows** **full**; **dipoles** on **polar** **bonds** where **required**; **intermediates** **named**.  
- **Isomers:** **unique** structures—**check** **duplicates**.

---

## Practical / data questions

- **Axes** with **units**; **line** vs **curve** of **best fit**.  
- **Uncertainty:** **readings**, **repeats**, **anomalies**.  
- **Improvements** must **match** **identified** **weakness**.

---

## Data booklet

Know **where** to find **periodicity**, **spectra**, **bond enthalpies**, **standard** **electrode** **potentials**—**saves** time in the **exam**.

---

## Revision cycle

1. **Timed** paper.  
2. **Mark** with **scheme**; **log** **errors** by **topic**.  
3. **Redo** **weak** **items** **next** day.  
4. **Repeat**.

---

## Checklist before the exam

- [ ] **All** **required** **practical** **skills** **reviewed**.  
- [ ] **Organic** **reagent** **conditions** **summarised** on **one** **sheet**.  
- [ ] **Physical** **formulae** **memorised** (**including** **log** forms if **pH**).  
- [ ] **Two** **full** **papers** **this** week **under** **exam** **conditions**.

**Now attempt one **structured** paper **without** notes.**
