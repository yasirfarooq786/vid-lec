# Complete Lecture: Sulfur (Cambridge International AS & A Level Chemistry 9701)

**Sulfur** allotropes, **oxides**, **oxoacids**, and the **Contact process** for **sulfuric acid**—core **inorganic** **9701** content. Programme: [Cambridge 9701](https://www.cambridgeinternational.org/programmes-and-qualifications/cambridge-international-as-and-a-level-chemistry-9701/).

## Elemental sulfur

**Rhombic** and **monoclinic** **S₈** rings; **plastic sulfur** on **rapid** **cooling** of **molten** **S**.

---

## Hydrogen sulfide H₂S

**Toxic**; **weak acid** in water; **reducing agent** (**S²⁻**/ **H₂S** oxidised to **S** or **higher** **ox. states**).

---

## Sulfur dioxide SO₂

**Bent** molecule; **acidic oxide** → **sulfurous acid** **equilibrium** in water.

**Redox:** **both** oxidised to **SO₄²⁻** and reduced to **S** (depending on reagent)—**bleach** action with **dyes** (**reducing** in **some** contexts).

**Environmental:** **fossil** fuels **S** content / **roasting** **sulfides** → **acid rain** precursor.

---

## Sulfur trioxide SO₃

**Electrophilic**; **reacts violently** with **water** → **H₂SO₄** (**industrially** **absorbed** in **98% H₂SO₄** as **oleum** process step).

---

## Sulfuric acid manufacture — Contact process (outline)

1. **Burn S** or **roast** **sulfide** ores → **SO₂**.  
2. **Catalytic oxidation:** **2SO₂ + O₂ ⇌ 2SO₃** (**V₂O₅** **catalyst**, **~450 °C**, **moderate** **pressure** to **favour** **rate** / **equilibrium** trade-off).  
3. **Absorb SO₃** in **conc. H₂SO₄** → **oleum**; **dilute** to **product** (**not** **direct** **water**—**too** **violent** / **mist**).

**Conditions** justified by **rate**, **equilibrium** (**exothermic** forward—**lower T** favours **SO₃** but **slows** reaction), **catalyst** **cost**.

---

## Uses of sulfuric acid

**Fertilisers** (**ammonium** **sulfate**, **phosphate** **production**), **detergents**, **dyes**, **refining**, **batteries** (**electrolyte**).

---

## Common misconceptions

- **SO₂** in **water** is **not** **pure** **H₂SO₃** as **isolated** **stable** **substance**—**equilibrium** mixture.  
- **Contact** plant **does not** bubble **SO₃** **straight** into **water** for **safety** and **efficiency**.
