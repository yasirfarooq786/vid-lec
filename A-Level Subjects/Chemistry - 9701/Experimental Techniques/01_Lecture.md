## Core apparatus

- **Volumetric glassware:** **volumetric flask** (single calibration mark), **pipette** (fixed volume), **burette** (variable volume to **0.05 cm³** precision typical).
- **Balance:** **2 d.p.** or as specified; **tare** for **containers**.
- **Thermometer / data logger** for **temperature** and **enthalpy** experiments.

---

## Titration (acid–base and redox)

**Procedure outline:**

1. **Standardise** if required (primary standard **acid** or **base**).
2. **Pipette** **aliquot** of **analyte** into **flask**; add **indicator** if **visual** endpoint.
3. **Titrate** from **burette** to **endpoint**; **repeat** for **concordant** titres (±0.10 cm³).
4. **Mean** titre → **moles** → **concentration** / **purity**.

**Indicators:** choose **pH** range **spanning** **equivalence** (e.g. methyl orange vs phenolphthalein for **strong–weak** combinations).

**Errors:** **parallax** when reading meniscus (**read bottom** of **meniscus** for **colourless** liquids), **overshoot**, **wet** pipette, **partial** drainage.

## Chromatography

- **Partition** between **mobile** and **stationary** phase.
- **Rf** = distance moved by **spot** ÷ distance moved by **solvent front** (0 < Rf < 1).
- **Paper / thin-layer** for **organic** **separation**; **identification** vs **standards**.

## Calorimetry (simple)

**q = mcΔT** for **solution** (approximate **c** ≈ **4.18 J g⁻¹ K⁻¹** for **dilute aqueous** unless given).

**ΔH** per **mole** requires **scaling** from **moles reacted**; **heat loss** to surroundings → **systematic** error (**too small |ΔH|** for **exothermic**).
