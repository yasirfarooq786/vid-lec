# Voice-Over Script: Chemical Energetics (Cambridge International AS & A Level Chemistry 9701)

**[Visual: Exothermic profile — products lower than reactants]**

**Narrator:**  
**Enthalpy** tracks **heat** at **constant pressure**. If **products** sit **lower** than **reactants**, **delta H** is **negative**—**exothermic**. **Combustion** is a classic **exothermic** class.

---

**[Visual: Hess cycle — alternate routes same start and end]**

**Narrator:**  
**Hess’s law** says **enthalpy change** depends only on **initial** and **final** states. Build **cycles** with **formation** data: **products’ formation enthalpies minus reactants’**—watch **stoichiometry** **coefficients**.

---

**[Visual: Bonds snapping (+) and forming (−)]**

**Narrator:**  
**Breaking** bonds **costs** energy—**positive** contribution. **Making** bonds **releases** energy. **Mean bond enthalpies** give **rough** **gas-phase** estimates, but **real** molecules vary.

---

**[Visual: Born–Haber loop for NaCl]**

**Narrator:**  
For **ionic** solids, **Born–Haber** links **atomisation**, **ionisation**, **electron affinity**, and **lattice** energy to **formation**. **Sign conventions** matter—always match your **data booklet**.

---
