# Complete Lecture: Air and Water (Cambridge International AS & A Level Chemistry 9701)

**Atmospheric** composition, **pollutants**, **catalytic converters**, and **water** **chemistry** (**hardness**, **treatment**, **environmental** impacts). ## Clean dry air (~volume)

~**78% N₂**, ~**21% O₂**, ~**1% Ar**, **trace CO₂** (~**0.04%**, rising), **noble gases**, **water vapour** (variable).

---

## Pollutants and photochemical smog

- **NOx** from **high T** **combustion** in **engines** (**N₂ + O₂**).  
- **Photochemical** reactions produce **ozone** and **peroxy** compounds at **ground level** (**harmful**).  
- **CO** from **incomplete** combustion (**toxic**—binds **haemoglobin**).

---

## Catalytic converter (three-way, outline)

**Reduces** **NOx** to **N₂**, **oxidises** **CO** and **unburnt** **hydrocarbons** to **CO₂** and **H₂O** using **Pt**, **Pd**, **Rh** on **ceramic** **honeycomb** (**surface** **catalysis**).

---

## Acid rain (synoptic)

**SO₂** / **SO₃** → **H₂SO₄** in **clouds**; **NOx** → **HNO₃**. **Damages** **buildings** (**carbonates**), **aquatic** **ecosystems**, **forests**.

**Flue gas desulfurisation** in **power stations** (**CaCO₃** / **CaO** **scrubbing**).

---

## Water hardness

- **Temporary:** **hydrogencarbonate** of **Ca²⁺/Mg²⁺**—removed by **boiling** (**CaCO₃** **precipitates**) or **lime** **soda**.  
- **Permanent:** **sulfates** / **chlorides**—**ion exchange** (**resin** **Na⁺** for **Ca²⁺**).

**Soap** forms **scum** with **hard** water (**Ca stearate** precipitate); **detergents** less affected.

---

## Water treatment (potable)

**Screening** → **coagulation** (**Al salts**) → **sedimentation** → **filtration** → **chlorination** (**disinfection**); **pH** adjustment.

---

## Eutrophication

**Nitrate** / **phosphate** **run-off** → **algal bloom** → **deoxygenation** when **organic** matter **decomposes** (**BOD**).

---

## Common misconceptions

- **Greenhouse effect** is **natural**; **enhanced** effect from **rising** **CO₂** / **CH₄** is **climate** concern.  
- **Ozone** **good** in **stratosphere**, **undesirable** **pollutant** at **ground** level.
