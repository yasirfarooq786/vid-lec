## 1. Roots of polynomials and symmetric functions

For **ax³ + bx² + cx + d = 0** with roots **α, β, γ:**

**α + β + γ = −b/a**  
**αβ + βγ + γα = c/a**  
**αβγ = −d/a**

**Substitutions** to find **equation** with roots **α²**, **1/α**, **α+1**, etc. — express **new** **elementary symmetric sums** in terms of **old**.

## 2. Rational functions

**f(x) = P(x)/Q(x)** — **vertical asymptotes** where **Q = 0** (unless **cancelling** factor); **horizontal** / **oblique** asymptotes from **degree** comparison.

**Partial fractions** with **repeated** / **quadratic** factors for **integration** and **series**.

---

## 3. Proof by induction

- **Statement P(n)**; **base** case; **inductive step** **P(k) ⇒ P(k+1)**; **conclusion** **∀n ∈ ℕ**.  
- **Strong induction** variant where **exam** requires.

---

## 4. Maclaurin’s series

**f(x) = f(0) + f′(0)x + f″(0)x²/2! + …**

**Standard expansions:** **eˣ**, **sin x**, **cos x**, **ln(1+x)**, **(1+x)ⁿ** (|**x**|<1 for **binomial** **fractional** **n**).

**Uses:** **approximation**, **limits**, **combining** series (valid **within** **radius** of **convergence** where **studied**).

---

## 5. Hyperbolic functions

**sinh x = (eˣ − e⁻ˣ)/2**, **cosh x = (eˣ + e⁻ˣ)/2**, **tanh x = sinh x/cosh x**

**Identities:** **cosh²x − sinh²x ≡ 1** (compare **trig** **Pythagoras**).

**Derivatives:** **d/dx(sinh x) = cosh x**, **d/dx(cosh x) = sinh x**, **d/dx(tanh x) = sech²x**.

**Inverse** **arsinh**, **arcosh**, **artanh** — **logarithmic** forms from **syllabus**.

---

## 6. First-order differential equations

**Separable:** **dy/dx = f(x)g(y)** → **∫ dy/g(y) = ∫ f(x) dx**

**Linear first-order:** **dy/dx + P(x)y = Q(x)** — **integrating factor** **μ = exp(∫P dx)**.

**Initial conditions** fix **constant**.

---

## 7. Second-order linear ODE (homogeneous, constant coefficients)

**ay″ + by′ + cy = 0** — **auxiliary equation** **am² + bm + c = 0**.

**Distinct real** roots → **Ae^m₁x + Be^m₂x**; **repeated** → **(A+Bx)e^mx**; **complex** **p ± iq** → **e^px(A cos qx + B sin qx)**.

*(Particular integral for **non-homogeneous** — **syllabus** depth.)*

---

## 8. Improper integrals and limits (synoptic)

**∫[a,∞) f(x) dx = lim(R→∞) ∫[a,R] f(x) dx** — **convergence** tests **awareness**.

## Common mistakes

- **Maclaurin:** **factorial** in **denominator**; **sign** errors in **alternating** series.  
- **ODE:** **forget** **+C** before **applying** **initial** **condition**.  
- **Induction** **algebra** — **false** **assumption** that **pattern** **equals** **proof**.
