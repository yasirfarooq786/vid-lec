# Voice-Over Script: Algebra and Graphs (Cambridge International AS & A Level Further Mathematics 9231)

**[Visual: Cubic with roots α, β, γ — sum and product formulas]**

**Narrator:**  
**Vieta’s relations** link **coefficients** to **sums** and **products** of **roots**. In **9231** you **manipulate** those to build **new** equations whose roots are **squares**, **reciprocals**, or **shifts** of the **original** roots.

---

**[Visual: Rational graph — vertical asymptotes, horizontal asymptote]**

**Narrator:**  
**Rational functions** behave like **polynomial** **ratio**. **Vertical** **asymptotes** come from **zeros** of the **denominator** after **cancelling** **common** factors. Compare **degrees** for **end** **behaviour**.

---

**[Visual: Maclaurin building sine from derivatives at zero]**

**Narrator:**  
**Maclaurin** series **encode** functions as **infinite** **polynomials** from **derivatives at zero**. **Combine** known **series** to **expand** **products** or **compositions** where **valid**.

---

**[Visual: cosh and sinh graphs — cosh even, sinh odd]**

**Narrator:**  
**Hyperbolic** **cosine** is **even**; **hyperbolic** **sine** is **odd**. They satisfy **cosh squared minus sinh squared equals one**—the **unit** **hyperbola** plays the role the **circle** plays for **ordinary** **trig**.

---

**[Visual: Separable DE — dy/dx = xy, separate and integrate]**

**Narrator:**  
**Separable** **differential** equations **split** **y** and **x** to **opposite** sides then **integrate**. **Linear** **first** order needs an **integrating factor** **e to the integral P d x**.

---

**[Visual: Auxiliary equation m² + 2m + 5 = 0 → complex roots]**

**Narrator:**  
**Second** order **constant** coefficient **ODEs** reduce to a **quadratic** **auxiliary** equation. **Complex** roots give **oscillating** **solutions** with **exponential** **envelope**.
