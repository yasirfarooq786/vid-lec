## 1. Paper structure (confirm for your series)

**9231** typically includes **Further Pure** papers and optional **Further Mechanics** / **Further Probability & Statistics** depending on **entry**. **Check** your **current** **syllabus** for **paper** **codes**, **duration**, and **total** **marks**.

**9709** **Pure** is **assumed** **background** — **9231** **questions** often **chain** **ideas** (**ODE** + **Maclaurin**; **vectors** + **matrices**).

---

## 2. Mark scheme culture

- **Method** marks for **correct** **structure** even if **algebra** **slips**.  
- **Accuracy** marks **often** **follow** **method**.  
- **B** marks for **independent** **statements** (**conclusions**, **interpretations**).  
- **C** marks (**communication**) in **some** **sessions** — **clear** **logic**, **defined** **notation**.

**Show** **key** **steps**: **characteristic** **equation** for **ODE**, **det(A−λI)** for **eigenvalues**, **induction** **assumption** **explicit**.

---

## 3. Command words (Further)

- **Prove / show that:** **logical** **sequence** — **no** **gaps** **examiner** **cannot** **fill**.  
- **Hence or otherwise:** **“Hence”** path usually **shorter** — **penalty** for **ignoring** **previous** **part**.  
- **In exact form:** **surds**, **π**, **e**, **fractions** — **avoid** **premature** **decimals**.  
- **Sketch:** **asymptotes**, **behaviour**, **key** **points** — **calculator** **graph** **for** **planning** **only**.

---

## 4. Time and stamina

**Further** papers are **dense**. **Target** **easier** **marks** **first** in **multi-part** questions; **return** to **proof** **heavy** **sections**.  
**Rough** guide: **≈ 1 mark per minute** — **calibrate** with **timed** **mocks**.

---

## 5. Formula booklet fluency

**Locate** **fast:** **Maclaurin** **standard** **series**, **trig** / **hyperbolic** **identities**, **normal** / **t** / **χ²** **tables**, **integration** **shortcuts**.

---

## 6. Calculator

- **Complex** **mode** for **roots** / **verification** (where **permitted**).  
- **Matrix** **mode** for **checking** **inverse** / **multiply** — **still** **show** **manual** **working** if **question** **asks**.  
- **Store** **full** **precision**; **round** **final** **answer** **only**.

---

## 7. Revision cycle

1. **Full** paper **timed**.  
2. **Mark** with **scheme**; **tag** **topic** (**eigenvalues**, **polar** **area**, **χ²** **df**).  
3. **Redo** **missed** questions **cold** next day.  
4. **Condense** **errors** into **one-page** **“panic”** **sheet**.
