# Voice-Over Script: Using Past Papers (Cambridge International AS & A Level Further Mathematics 9231)

**[Visual: 9231 papers — Further Pure 1, 2, optional applied]**

**Narrator:**  
**Further Mathematics 9231** papers are **long** and **linked**. **Pure** questions often **build**—part **three** uses part **one**’s **equation** or **matrix**. **Read** the **whole** question before you **commit** **algebra**.

---

**[Visual: “Show that” with tick boxes for each logical line]**

**Narrator:**  
**Proof** questions need **visible** **logic**. **Induction** must **name** the **inductive hypothesis**. **“Show that”** means **every** **equals** sign should be **justified**—**expand**, **factor**, **cite** **identity**.

---

**[Visual: Formula booklet open — tabs on series, distributions]**

**Narrator:**  
The **booklet** is **not** a **crutch**—it is a **speed** tool. **Tab** **Maclaurin**, **integrals**, and **critical** **values** so you **never** **hunt** during the **exam**.

---

**[Visual: Split screen — leave hard proof, score marks on stats]**

**Narrator:**  
If a **proof** **stalls**, **move** on. **Further** applied papers mix **short** **computational** marks with **long** **arguments**—**bank** **easy** **marks** **first**.

---

**[Visual: Error log — eigenvector normalisation, χ² df]**

**Narrator:**  
After each **mock**, **log** **repeat** mistakes: **wrong** **degrees** of **freedom**, **missing** **half** in **polar** **area**, **forgetting** **+C** in **ODE**. **Drill** those **patterns** **only**.

---

**[Visual: Checklist — structure, hence, booklet, time, error log]**

**Narrator:**  
**9231** rewards **consistent** **method** over **clever** **shortcuts** you **cannot** **explain**. **Practise** **explaining** **while** **solving**. Good luck.
