## 1. Inverse trigonometric functions

**arcsin**, **arccos**, **arctan** — **restricted** **domains** for **one-one** **behaviour**.

**sin(arcsin x) = x** for **x ∈ [−1,1]**; **arcsin(sin θ) = θ** only for **θ** in **principal** **range**.

**Differentiation:** **d/dx(arcsin x) = 1/√(1−x²)**; **d/dx(arctan x) = 1/(1+x²)** (**formula booklet**).

---

## 2. Further identities

**sin A ± sin B = 2 sin((A±B)/2) cos((A∓B)/2)** (sum-to-product)  
**cos A + cos B = 2 cos((A+B)/2) cos((A−B)/2)**

Use to **solve** **equations** and **integrate** **products** of **sines/cosines**.

---

## 3. Hyperbolic ↔ circular (Osborn’s rule)

**Replace** **sin** by **sinh**, **cos** by **cosh** with **appropriate** **sign** changes for **products** of **two** **sinh** terms — **systematic** way to **remember** **hyperbolic** **identities** from **trig**.

---

## 4. t-substitution (Weierstrass) — awareness

**t = tan(θ/2)** gives **sin θ = 2t/(1+t²)**, **cos θ = (1−t²)/(1+t²)**, **dθ = 2/(1+t²) dt** — converts **rational** **trig** **integrals** to **rational** **t** **integrals**.

---

## 5. Small-angle refinements

**Maclaurin:** **sin x = x − x³/6 + …**, **cos x = 1 − x²/2 + …** — **approximation** and **limit** problems.

---

## Common mistakes

- **Inverse** **trig** **ranges** — **wrong** **branch** loses **solutions**.  
- **Differentiating** **arcsin** — **missing** **chain** rule **inside**.
