# Voice-Over Script: Geometry (Cambridge International AS & A Level Further Mathematics 9231)

**[Visual: Three points P Q R — vector PQ parallel to PR]**

**Narrator:**  
**Vectors** make **geometry** **algebraic**. Points are **collinear** if the **vector** from **P** to **Q** is a **scalar** **multiple** of **P** to **R**. **No** diagram guesswork—**write** the **vector** **equation**.

---

**[Visual: Plane with normal vector n — dot product with position]**

**Narrator:**  
A **plane** is **all** points whose **position vector** **dot** **normal** equals a **constant**. **Two** planes **meet** at an **angle** found from **their** **normals**—**cosine** of the **angle** between **normal** vectors.

---

**[Visual: Skew lines in 3D — no intersection, not parallel]**

**Narrator:**  
In **three** dimensions, lines can **miss** each other **without** being **parallel**—**skew**. The **shortest** **distance** uses the **cross product** of **direction** vectors and a **vector** **joining** **one** point on each **line**.
