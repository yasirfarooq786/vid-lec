## 1. Vector proof in the plane and space

**Collinearity:** **PQ = λPR** for **scalar** **λ** → **P, Q, R** collinear.

**Parallelism:** **direction** vectors **scalar multiples**.

**Perpendicularity:** **a · b = 0** (non-zero vectors).

**Midpoint** **M** of **AB:** **OM = ½(OA + OB)**.

---

## 2. Equation of a plane (3D)

**Scalar form:** **r · n = p** (**n** **normal** vector)  
**Cartesian:** **ax + by + cz = d**

**Angle** between **planes:** **cos θ = |n₁ · n₂| / (|n₁||n₂|)** (acute angle convention).

**Line–plane intersection:** substitute **parametric line** **r = a + λb** into **plane** equation.

---

## 3. Distance from point to plane

**|ax₀ + by₀ + cz₀ − d| / √(a² + b² + c²)** for **plane** **ax + by + cz = d**.

---

## 4. Shortest distance between skew lines

**L₁: r = a₁ + λb₁**, **L₂: r = a₂ + μb₂** (**b₁** not **parallel** **b₂**).

**Distance** = **|(a₂ − a₁) · (b₁ × b₂)| / |b₁ × b₂|**  
(**scalar triple product** magnitude over **area** of **parallelogram** from **b₁, b₂**)
