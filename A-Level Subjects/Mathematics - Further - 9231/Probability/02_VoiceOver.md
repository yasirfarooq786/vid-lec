# Voice-Over Script: Probability (Cambridge International AS & A Level Further Mathematics 9231)

**[Visual: E(aX+b) = aE(X)+b — number line stretch]**

**Narrator:**  
**Expectation** is **linear**—**scaling** and **shifting** **pull** **outside** as **constants**. **Variance** **ignores** **shifts** but **squares** **scales**: **variance of a X** is **a squared** times **variance of X**.

---

**[Visual: Poisson bar chart — mean equals variance]**

**Narrator:**  
**Poisson** counts **rare** **events** in a **fixed** **window**. Its **mean** and **variance** both equal **lambda**. **Add** **independent** **Poisson** counts and **lambdas** **add**.

---

**[Visual: Geometric — first success after failures]**

**Narrator:**  
The **geometric** distribution models **waiting** for the **first** **success**. **Memoryless** property (**conditional** **probability** **restarts**) appears in **exam** **theory** questions.

---

**[Visual: Uniform density flat top from a to b]**

**Narrator:**  
**Continuous** **uniform** spreads **probability** **evenly** over an **interval**. **Density** is **one** over **length**; **expectation** is the **midpoint**.

---

**[Visual: G_X(t) power series coefficients p0, p1, p2…]**

**Narrator:**  
**Probability generating functions** **encode** the **whole** **distribution** in a **power** **series**. **Multiplying** **PGFs** **adds** **independent** **random** **variables**—like **exponents** **adding** for **Poisson**.
