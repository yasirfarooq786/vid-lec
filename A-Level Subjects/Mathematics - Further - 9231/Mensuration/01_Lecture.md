## 1. Polar area (core)

**A = ½ ∫[α,β] r² dθ**

For **r = f(θ)**, **identify** **α, β** where **curve** **traced** **once** without **overlap**.

---

## 2. Arc length

**Cartesian:** **s = ∫ √(1 + (dy/dx)²) dx**  
**Parametric:** **s = ∫ √((dx/dt)² + (dy/dt)²) dt**  
**Polar:** **s = ∫ √(r² + (dr/dθ)²) dθ**

---

## 3. Volume of revolution

**y = f(x)** about **x-axis:** **V = π ∫[a,b] y² dx**  
About **y-axis:** **V = π ∫[c,d] x² dy** or **shell** method (**2π ∫ x f(x) dx**) per **syllabus**.

**Parametric:** substitute **x(t), y(t)** with **correct** **limits**.

---

## 4. Surface area of revolution (if required)

**S = 2π ∫ y √(1 + (dy/dx)²) dx** (about **x-axis**, **y ≥ 0**)

---

## 5. Centroid and centre of mass (synoptic with Mechanics)

**x̄ = (∫ x f(x) dx) / (∫ f(x) dx)** for **lamina** under **curve** (**uniform** **density**).

---

## Common mistakes

- **Polar** **area:** **forgot** **½** **factor**.  
- **Arc length:** **limits** must match **parameter** **range**.  
- **Revolution** axis — **square** the **correct** **function**.
