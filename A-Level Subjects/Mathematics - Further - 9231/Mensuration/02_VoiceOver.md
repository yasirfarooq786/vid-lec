# Voice-Over Script: Mensuration (Cambridge International AS & A Level Further Mathematics 9231)

**[Visual: Polar petal r = sin 3θ — shaded sectors]**

**Narrator:**  
**Polar** **area** stacks **infinitesimal** **sectors** of **area** **half r squared d theta**. **Choose** **limits** so you **sweep** the **region** **exactly** **once**—**petals** and **loops** need **care**.

---

**[Visual: Arc length element ds² = dx² + dy²]**

**Narrator:**  
**Arc length** is **integral** of **root one plus dy by dx squared**. In **parametric** form, **dx by dt** and **dy by dt** go **inside** the **square root** with respect to **t**.

---

**[Visual: Solid of revolution — disks stacked]**

**Narrator:**  
**Revolving** a **graph** about an **axis** gives **pi** times **integral** of **radius squared**—the **radius** is **perpendicular** **distance** to the **axis**. **Parametric** curves need **limits** in **t**.

---

**[Visual: Surface of revolution — band around vase]**

**Narrator:**  
**Surface** **area** adds up **thin** **bands**—**two pi y ds** for **rotation** about the **x-axis**. **ds** is the **arc** **element**, **not** just **dx**.

---
