## 1. Scalar (dot) product

**a · b = |a||b| cos θ = a₁b₁ + a₂b₂ + a₃b₃**

**Perpendicular** ⟺ **a · b = 0**.

---

## 2. Vector (cross) product (3D)

**a × b** — **vector** **perpendicular** to **a** and **b**, **magnitude** **|a||b| sin θ**, **right-hand** rule **direction**.

**Component formula** from **determinant** of **i, j, k** **rows** with **a**, **b**.

**a × a = 0**; **b × a = −a × b**.

---

## 3. Scalar triple product

**a · (b × c)** — **volume** of **parallelepiped** (signed); **= 0** ⟺ **coplanar** vectors.

---

## 4. Matrices (Further)

**Multiplication**, **determinant** **det A**, **inverse** **A⁻¹** (**2×2** and **3×3** as examined).

**Linear transformation:** **X′ = MX** (**column** vectors).

**Composition:** **M₂M₁** applies **M₁** **first** if **v′ = M₂M₁v** (confirm **convention** in **question**).

## 5. Eigenvalues and eigenvectors

**Av = λv** (**v ≠ 0**) ⟺ **det(A − λI) = 0** (**characteristic equation**).

**Diagonalisation** **A = PDP⁻¹** where **D** **diagonal**, **columns** of **P** **eigenvectors** (**independent**).

**Applications:** **powers** **Aⁿ**, **coupled** **systems**, **stability** (**synoptic**).

---

## 6. Rotations and reflections

**2D rotation** **θ:** **[[cos θ, −sin θ], [sin θ, cos θ]]**  
**Reflection** in **line** through **origin** — **matrix** from **syllabus** / **derivation**.

- **Cross product** **order** — **sign** **flip**.  
- **Eigenvector** **not** unique — **any** **non-zero** **scalar** **multiple** **valid**.  
- **Characteristic** equation **degree** = **matrix** **size**.
