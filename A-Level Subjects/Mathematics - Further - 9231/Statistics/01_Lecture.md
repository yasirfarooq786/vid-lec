## 1. Continuous random variables

**PDF** **f(x)** with **∫ f(x) dx = 1** over **support**.

**P(a ≤ X ≤ b) = ∫[a,b] f(x) dx**

**E(X) = ∫ x f(x) dx**, **Var(X) = E(X²) − [E(X)]²**

**CDF** **F(x) = P(X ≤ x)** with **F′(x) = f(x)** (where **differentiable**).

---

## 2. Normal distribution **N(μ, σ²)**

**Standardise** **Z = (X − μ)/σ**; **use** **tables** / **calculator**.

**Sample mean** **X̄** of **n** **i.i.d.** **N(μ, σ²):** **X̄ ~ N(μ, σ²/n)**

**Central Limit Theorem:** **X̄** **approximately** **normal** for **large n** even if **X** **not** **normal** (**conditions**).

---

## 3. t-distribution

When **σ** **unknown**, **estimate** **s** from **sample**; **T = (X̄ − μ)/(s/√n)** has **t**-distribution with **ν = n − 1** **degrees of freedom** (**one-sample** **mean** test / **CI**).

---

## 4. Confidence intervals

**μ** (**σ known**): **X̄ ± z_(α/2) × σ/√n**  
**μ** (**σ unknown**): **X̄ ± t_(α/2) × s/√n**

**Interpret:** **interval** **contains** **μ** with **stated** **confidence** (**long-run** **frequency** **interpretation**).

---

## 5. Hypothesis tests

**H₀** vs **H₁**; **test statistic**; **p-value** or **critical** **region** at **significance** **α**.

**Type I** (**α**): **reject** **true** **H₀**  
**Type II** (**β**): **accept** **false** **H₀**; **power = 1 − β**

---

## 6. χ² tests

**Goodness of fit:** **χ² = Σ (O − E)²/E** with **degrees of freedom** **= cells − 1 − parameters estimated** (**rule** from **syllabus**).

**Independence** in **contingency** **table:** **df = (r−1)(c−1)**

**Expected** **frequencies** **≥ 5** (or **pool** **cells**).

---

## 7. Correlation and regression (Further depth)

**Product-moment** **r**; **test** **ρ = 0** using **t**-statistic (**formula** **booklet**).

**Unbiased** **estimators:** **sample** **variance** **divisor** **n − 1**.

---

## Common mistakes

- **Confusing** **σ** and **s** in **standard error**.  
- **χ²:** **wrong** **degrees** of **freedom** after **estimating** **parameters**.  
- **One-tailed** vs **two-tailed** **critical** **values**.
