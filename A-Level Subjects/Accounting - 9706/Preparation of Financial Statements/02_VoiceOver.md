# Voice-Over Script: Preparation of Financial Statements (Cambridge International AS & A Level Accounting 9706)

**[Visual: Statement of profit or loss building from revenue down to profit]**

**Narrator:**  
The **statement of profit or loss** tells the **story** of the **year**: **sales**, **cost of sales**, **gross profit**, then **overheads** and **other** income, down to **profit for the year**. Learn the **order** so you can **draft** from an **adjusted** trial balance **without** forgetting lines like **depreciation** or **doubtful debts**.

---

**[Visual: SOFP columns — non-current assets, current assets, liabilities, equity]**

**Narrator:**  
The **statement of financial position** is a **snapshot**. **Non-current assets** first, then **current assets** and **current liabilities**, then **non-current liabilities**. **Net assets** must **tie** to **capital** or **shareholders’ equity**. **Receivables** are **after** **allowance**; **inventory** is **lower** of **cost** and **NRV** when that rule applies.

---

**[Visual: Partnership — appropriation waterfall to three partners]**

**Narrator:**  
For **partnerships**, after **profit** you build an **appropriation account**: **interest on capital**, **salaries** if any, then **residual** profit by **agreed ratio**. **Drawings** go through **current** accounts or **capital**—follow the **question** format.

---

**[Visual: Company — share capital + retained earnings box]**

**Narrator:**  
**Limited companies** add **share capital** and **reserves**. **Dividends** belong in **changes in equity** thinking—not as a **P&L** **expense**. Use **past papers** to match **Cambridge** **layout** preferences.

---

**[Visual: Incomplete records — working T-accounts on scratch paper]**

**Narrator:**  
**Incomplete records** questions reward **method**. Rebuild **missing** **sales** or **purchases** using **margins**, **cash** totals, and **receivables** **equations**. Always **show** **workings**.

---

**[Visual: Checklist — P&L, SOFP, partnership, company basics, incomplete]**

**Narrator:**  
Practise **full** papers under **time** limits with **neat** **titles** and **labels**. Next: **accounting principles** behind those **choices**.
