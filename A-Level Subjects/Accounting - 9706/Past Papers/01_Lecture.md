# Complete Lecture: Using Past Papers (Cambridge International AS & A Level Accounting 9706)

This module supports **examination technique** for **9706**—not new accounting content. Official materials: [Cambridge 9706](https://www.cambridgeinternational.org/programmes-and-qualifications/cambridge-international-as-and-a-level-accounting-9706/) and your centre’s access to **past papers** and **mark schemes**.

---

## Typical assessment structure (confirm for your session)

| Paper | Focus |
|-------|--------|
| **Paper 1** | **Multiple choice** — quick **recall** and **short** application |
| **Paper 2** | **Structured written** — **calculations**, **ledger** work, **partial** statements |
| **Paper 3** | **Structured** (A Level continuation) — often **larger** **integrations** |

**Times** and **marks** vary by series—read the **front** of your **specimen** paper every year.

---

## Command words

- **Prepare / draft:** complete **statement** or **account** with **correct** **format**.
- **Explain:** **because** … **therefore** …—link **rule** to **effect** on **profit** or **position**.
- **Calculate / show your workings:** **numbers** only **without** workings **risk** **full** marks.
- **Advise / recommend:** **decision** + **reason** from **ratios** or **liquidity**.
- **State / define:** **short**, **precise** **definitions**.

---

## Mark scheme habits

- **One mark per valid point** in **narrative**; **own figure rule** (OFR)—**follow-through** from **earlier** **errors** if **method** is **sound**.
- **Labels** on **statements** (**$**, **titles**, **dates**) may carry **marks**.
- **Balancing** **T-accounts** and **trial balance**—show **care** with **dr/cr**.

---

## Time strategy

- **Scan** whole paper; **rank** by **confidence** if allowed (where **not** **sequential**).
- **Overspend** on **Question 1** and **rush** **ratios** at the end—**common** mistake. **Allocate** minutes by **marks**.

---

## Accuracy habits

- **Copy** numbers from **question** **slowly**; **round** only as **instructed**.
- **Bracket** negatives clearly; **show** **cross-casts** on **day books**.
- **Bank reconciliation**—**two** **separate** **parts**: **adjusted cash book** + **reconciliation statement**.

---

## Revision cycle

1. **Timed** **full** paper.
2. **Mark** with **scheme**; **tabulate** **recurring** **errors** (e.g. **disposal**, **allowance** **movement**).
3. **Redo** **only** **weak** topics with **notes closed**.
4. **Repeat** weekly.

---

## Integrity

Use past papers for **practice** under your **school’s** **academic integrity** rules; **specimen** and **published** resources are for **learning**, not **misrepresentation** of **assessed** work.
