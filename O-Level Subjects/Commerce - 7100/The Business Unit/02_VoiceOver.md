**Narrator:** So, why bother? First up: **Laser Focus**. BUs allow a company to zero in on specific products, services, customer segments, or geographic markets. No more trying to be everything to everyone!

**Narrator:** Next, **Agility and Speed**. By decentralizing decisions and resources, BUs can react way faster to market changes, innovate quicker, and outmaneuver the competition. Think nimble, not lumbering!

**Narrator:** And for accountability? BUs often have their own profit and loss (P&L) responsibility. This means clear ownership and a direct link between effort and results. Super motivating, right?
