# Complete Lecture: Past Papers (Cambridge IGCSE Pakistan Studies 0448 — Paper 1)

This course folder follows **Cambridge IGCSE Pakistan Studies (0448) Paper 1: The history and culture of Pakistan**. It is **not** the same qualification as **Cambridge IGCSE History (0470)**—see [Pakistan Studies (0448)](https://www.cambridgeinternational.org/programmes-and-qualifications/cambridge-igcse-pakistan-studies-0448/) and, for comparison, [History (0470)](https://www.cambridgeinternational.org/programmes-and-qualifications/cambridge-igcse-history-0470/).

---

## 1. Paper 1 question types you should rehearse

- **Narrative** with chronology; **analytical** “why/how far”; **source-based** tasks if included in your series.

---

## 2. Mark-scheme habits

- **Define** terms; **support** with **specific** detail (names/dates/places).
- **Evaluate** with a second factor or limitation—avoid one-sided essays.

---

## 3. Timing and planning

- For long questions: **5 minutes** plan, write **PEEL** paragraphs, leave **2 minutes** to check dates.

---

## Revision checklist

- [ ] One timed paper in exam conditions.
- [ ] Highlight **missed specificity** (vague “they” → name the actor).
- [ ] Build a **glossary** of 30 terms with one-line definitions.
