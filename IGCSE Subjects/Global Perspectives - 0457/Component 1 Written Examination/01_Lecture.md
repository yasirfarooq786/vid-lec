This material supports **Cambridge IGCSE Global Perspectives (0457)**—skills for enquiry into global issues, structured argument, and assessed components. ## 1. What Component 1 tests

- Application of **skills** under timed conditions: comprehension, analysis, evaluation—**exact formats** follow your **specimen** papers.

---

## 2. Exam technique

- **Annotate** stimulus in the reading time: audience, purpose, gaps.
- Plan **paragraph order** before you commit—especially for multi-part questions.

---

## 3. Mark awareness

- Match **marks** to **depth**: two marks need precision; eight marks need **layers** (perspectives + evidence + evaluation).

---

## Common misconceptions

- Ignoring **stimulus boundaries**—use the resource, do not drift into generic essays.

---

## Revision checklist

- [ ] Complete one **timed** specimen section without notes, then mark with criteria.
- [ ] Build a **phrase bank** for evaluation (effective/limited because…).
- [ ] Practise **synoptic** links between two issues in one question if examined.
