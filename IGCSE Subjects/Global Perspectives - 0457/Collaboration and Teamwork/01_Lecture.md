---

## 1. Roles and routines

- Agree **deadlines**, **shared doc hygiene**, and **meeting norms** early.

---

## 2. Constructive disagreement

- Disagree on **ideas**, not personalities; summarise others’ views before you respond.

---

## 3. Equity of contribution

- Track tasks visibly; rotate **note-taker** and **spokesperson** roles.

---

## Common misconceptions

- Letting one person carry the **presentation** without shared understanding—examiners can probe **whole** team.

---

## Revision checklist

- [ ] Create a **RACI-style** mini chart for your team project.
- [ ] Run one **timed** rehearsal with questions from a “critical friend.”
- [ ] Archive meeting notes and **decisions** for your portfolio trail.
