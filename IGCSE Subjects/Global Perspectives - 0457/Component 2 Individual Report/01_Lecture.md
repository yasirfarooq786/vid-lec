# Complete Lecture: Component 2 Individual Report (Cambridge IGCSE Global Perspectives 0457)

This material supports **Cambridge IGCSE Global Perspectives (0457)**—skills for enquiry into global issues, structured argument, and assessed components. Programme: [Cambridge IGCSE Global Perspectives (0457)](https://www.cambridgeinternational.org/programmes-and-qualifications/cambridge-igcse-global-perspectives-0457/).

---

## 1. Purpose

- Extended enquiry showing **research**, **analysis**, **evaluation**, and **reflection** on a chosen global issue.

---

## 2. Structure habits

- Clear **introduction** (scope and question); **themed** middle sections; **integrated** reflection; **decisive** conclusion.

---

## 3. Ethics and safety

- Sensitive topics need **safeguarding** awareness; follow school ethics rules for interviews/surveys.

---

## Common misconceptions

- Pasting long quotes to hit word count—select **short** evidence and **explain** it.

---

## Revision checklist

- [ ] Draft a **one-page** outline approved by your teacher.
- [ ] Run a **peer review** for clarity and bias blind spots.
- [ ] Check bibliography format against the **syllabus** or centre guide.
