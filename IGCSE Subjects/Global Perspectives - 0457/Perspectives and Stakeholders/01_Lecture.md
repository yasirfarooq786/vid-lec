## 1. Perspectives

- A **perspective** is a viewpoint shaped by role, culture, incentives, and information.
- **Empathy** in GP means understanding interests accurately—not agreeing with every claim.

---

## 2. Stakeholders

- Governments, firms, communities, NGOs, international bodies, future generations—map **who gains/loses** and **why**.

---

## 3. Bias and standpoint

- **Selection bias** in news; **funding** influences in research; **cultural assumptions** in how problems are framed.

---

## Common misconceptions

- Treating one powerful voice as “neutral.” Ask: **who benefits** from this framing?

---

## Revision checklist

- [ ] Draw a **stakeholder wheel** for one issue.
- [ ] Write **two short paragraphs** arguing **different** reasonable positions.
- [ ] Find one source and state its **likely bias** in one sentence.
