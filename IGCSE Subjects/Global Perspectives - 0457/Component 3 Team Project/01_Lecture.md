## 1. Team outcome

- Collaborative enquiry with a **shared question** and **coherent** final product (format per syllabus).

---

## 2. Process evidence

- Keep logs of **decisions**, **sources**, and **draft iterations**—helps reflection and fairness.

---

## 3. Presentation skills (if assessed)

- Clear **signposting**, **time** discipline, **visual** support that reinforces—not repeats—speech.

---

## Common misconceptions

- “Equal slides = equal work.” Assessors look for **integrated** thinking, not cosmetic balance.

---

## Revision checklist

- [ ] Align team **sub-questions** to one umbrella question.
- [ ] Rehearse **hand-offs** between speakers until seamless.
- [ ] Prepare **two** backup answers to likely examiner questions.
