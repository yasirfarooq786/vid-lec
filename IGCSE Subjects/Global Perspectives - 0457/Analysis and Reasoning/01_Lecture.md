# Complete Lecture: Analysis and Reasoning (Cambridge IGCSE Global Perspectives 0457)

This material supports **Cambridge IGCSE Global Perspectives (0457)**—skills for enquiry into global issues, structured argument, and assessed components. Programme: [Cambridge IGCSE Global Perspectives (0457)](https://www.cambridgeinternational.org/programmes-and-qualifications/cambridge-igcse-global-perspectives-0457/).

---

## 1. Claims and reasons

- Separate **conclusion**, **reasons**, and **evidence**; show **links** explicitly (“This supports the idea because…”).

---

## 2. Causes and mechanisms

- Distinguish **proximate** vs **structural** causes; avoid single-factor stories unless the question demands it.

---

## 3. Counterarguments

- Name the **strongest** objection to your line and answer it—this strengthens analysis marks.

---

## Common misconceptions

- Listing facts without **explaining** how they support a point.

---

## Revision checklist

- [ ] Colour-highlight: **claim** / **reason** / **evidence** in a model paragraph.
- [ ] Write a **counterargument** paragraph for a past question.
- [ ] Practise **because/chains** without vague connectors (“so”, “obviously”).
