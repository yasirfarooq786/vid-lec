## 1. Techniques

- Integrate **x^n** (n ≠ −1); recognise **∫ f'(x) f(x)^n dx** patterns if taught.

## 2. Definite integrals

- Area under a curve (respecting sign); interpret **negative** area carefully.

## Common misconceptions

- Matrix multiplication **order** matters: **AB** vs **BA** is not generally interchangeable.
