## 1. Series

- nth term and sum formulas as taught; convergence ideas for infinite geometric series if included.

## 2. Binomial expansion

- **(a+b)^n** for positive integer **n**; general term **C(n,r)a^(n-r)b^r**.

## 3. Counting

- **nPr**, **nCr**; factorial principles; careful with restrictions.
